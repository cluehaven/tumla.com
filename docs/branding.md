# Tumla Branding Guidelines

This document outlines the visual identity and branding standards for Tumla.

## Logo Usage

### Placement
- The Tumla logo should appear at the top of all pages
- Logo should be left-aligned or centered in the header
- Maintain consistent placement across all pages

### Minimum Size
- Minimum logo height: 40px (mobile)
- Recommended logo height: 50-80px (desktop)
- Logo should never be scaled below minimum size

### Safe Space
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least 20% of the logo height
- Do not place text or other elements too close to the logo

### Logo Files
- Primary logo: `images/logo.png`
- Logo should link to homepage when clicked

## Colour Palette

### Primary Colours
- **Brand Orange**: `#FF6B2B`
  - Used for: Links, call-to-action elements, accents
  - RGB: rgb(255, 107, 43)

- **Text White**: `#FFFFFF`
  - Used for: Text on dark backgrounds, hero sections
  - RGB: rgb(255, 255, 255)

### Secondary Colours
- **Overlay Dark**: `rgba(20, 40, 60, 0.6)`
  - Used for: Hero section overlays
  - RGB: rgba(20, 40, 60, 0.6)

- **Footer Background**: `#FFFFFF`
  - Used for: Footer sections
  - RGB: rgb(255, 255, 255)

- **Footer Text Grey**: `#A0A0A0`
  - Used for: Footer text, legal text
  - RGB: rgb(160, 160, 160)

### Yearbook Pages
- **INSEAD Green**: `#006633`
  - Used for: Yearbook page backgrounds
  - RGB: rgb(0, 102, 51)

- **Brand Yellow/Gold**: `#FFC72C`
  - Used for: Links on yearbook pages
  - RGB: rgb(255, 199, 44)

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary Font**: Montserrat (Google Fonts)
- **Weights Used**: 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)

### Heading Hierarchy

#### H1 (Main Headings)
- Font size: 2.5rem - 3.5rem (desktop)
- Font weight: 700 (Bold)
- Letter spacing: 4-6px (for brand name)
- Text transform: Uppercase (for brand name)

#### H2 (Section Headings)
- Font size: 1.5rem (desktop)
- Font weight: 700 (Bold)
- Margin top: 30px
- Margin bottom: 15px

#### Body Text
- Font size: 1rem - 1.2rem
- Font weight: 300-400
- Line height: 1.6
- Color: #333333 (dark text) or #FFFFFF (white text on dark backgrounds)

### Responsive Typography
- Mobile (max-width: 768px): Reduce font sizes by approximately 20-30%
- Small mobile (max-width: 480px): Further reduce by 10-15%

## Tone of Voice

### Brand Voice Characteristics
- **Clear and Direct**: Communicate simply and without unnecessary jargon
- **Community-Focused**: Emphasize connection and support within MBA communities
- **Professional yet Approachable**: Maintain professionalism while being accessible
- **Student-Centric**: Always remember "We ourselves are the customer"

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice where possible
- Keep sentences concise
- Avoid overly formal language
- Focus on benefits to the MBA community
- Be transparent and honest

## Layout Standards

### Spacing
- Consistent padding: 20px (mobile), 40-60px (desktop)
- Section spacing: 40-60px between major sections
- Paragraph spacing: 15-25px between paragraphs

### Content Width
- Maximum content width: 800px (policy pages), 900px (main content), 1200px (full width sections)
- Center-aligned content containers

### Header
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Border bottom: 1px solid #E0E0E0
- Padding: 20px vertical
- Logo and text: Centered or left-aligned

### Footer
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Padding: 60px vertical (desktop), 40px (mobile)
- Text alignment: Center
- Links: Grey (#A0A0A0) with hover state to Brand Orange (#FF6B2B)

## Visual Consistency

### Page Types
1. **Home Page**: Hero section with background image, overlay, and centered content
2. **Policy Pages**: White background, header with logo, structured content
3. **Yearbook Pages**: Green background (#006633), white text, yellow/gold links

### Common Elements
- All pages should include:
  - Header with Tumla logo linking to homepage
  - Consistent footer with legal links
  - Same font family (Montserrat)
  - Consistent spacing and padding

## Implementation Notes

- All styles are defined in `style.css`
- Use semantic HTML5 elements
- Maintain accessibility standards (alt text for images, proper heading hierarchy)
- Ensure responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop
